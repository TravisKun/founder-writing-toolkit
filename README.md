## 🧩 What is founder-writing-toolkit?

The founder-writing-toolkit is a browser extension that helps you write emails and LinkedIn messages quickly and clearly. It works locally, so your messages stay private. This tool uses Claude for smart suggestions while you write in Gmail or LinkedIn. The extension runs on modern browsers like Chrome and Edge and installs with a single command.

You do not need any technical skills to use it. It is designed as a beta version to let you try out new writing tools while keeping your information safe.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- Work inside Gmail and LinkedIn to improve your writing.
- Local-first approach keeps your data on your computer.
- Powered by Claude for helpful text suggestions.
- Simple setup with a one-command installer.
- Compatible with Chrome (MV3) and Edge browsers.
- Designed to help you write clear, professional messages faster.
- Developer tools included for future updates.

## 🔧 How to Use founder-writing-toolkit

1. Open Gmail or LinkedIn in your browser.
2. Start writing an email or message.
3. The extension will offer suggestions based on your text.
4. Click suggestions to insert them directly into your message.
5. Use the toolbar of the extension to adjust settings like tone or length.
6. All your data stays on your device; the extension does not send your text online.
